Chicken Fried Rice

Chicken fried rice is a popular and delicious dish that combines tender pieces of chicken, cooked rice, and a medley of vegetables, all stir-fried together in a flavorful sauce. Here's a brief description of the recipe:


List Of Ingredients :

  • Cooked rice (preferably cold or day-old)

  • Boneless chicken breast or thigh, diced

  • Vegetables (common choices include carrots, peas, bell peppers, and onions)

  • Garlic and ginger, minced

  • Soy sauce

  • Oyster sauce (optional)
  • Eggs

  • Vegetable oil

  • Salt and pepper to taste

  • Green onions for garnish

Instructions For preparation

  1. Prep Ingredients: Start by cooking the rice in advance and letting it cool. Cut the chicken into small pieces and chop the vegetables into bite-sized chunks.

  2. Cook Chicken: Heat some oil in a wok or large skillet over high heat. Add the chicken pieces and cook until they turn golden brown and are fully cooked through. Remove the chicken from the pan and set it aside.

  3. Saute Vegetables: In the same pan, add a bit more oil if needed. Stir-fry the minced garlic and ginger for about 30 seconds until fragrant. Then, add the chopped vegetables and stir-fry until they are tender but still crisp.

  4. Scramble Eggs: Push the vegetables to the side of the pan and add a bit of oil to the empty space. Crack the eggs into the pan and scramble them until they are fully cooked. Then, mix them with the sauteed vegetables.

  5. Add Rice and Chicken: Add the cold, cooked rice to the pan with the vegetables and eggs. Mix everything together, breaking up any clumps of rice. Return the cooked chicken to the pan.

  6. Season: soy sauce and oyster sauce (if using) over the rice and chicken mixture. Stir-fry everything together until the rice is evenly coated with the sauce. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  7. Finish and Garnish: Continue to stir-fry for a few more minutes until the rice is heated through and has a slightly crispy texture. Garnish with chopped green onions.

  8. Serve: Transfer the chicken fried rice to serving plates or bowls, and enjoy your delicious homemade meal!


Chicken Noodles

Chicken noodles is a delightful and comforting dish that combines tender chicken pieces, vegetables, and egg noodles in a flavorful broth or sauce. Here's a brief description of the recipe:

List Of Ingredients:

  • Boneless chicken breast or thigh, thinly sliced

  • Egg noodles

  • Chicken broth or water

  • Vegetables common choices include carrots, celery, and green onions

  • Garlic and ginger, minced

  • Soy sauce

  • Sesame oil (optional)

  • Eggs

  • Vegetable oil

  • Salt and pepper to tas

Instructions For Preparation

  1. Boil Noodles: Begin by boiling the egg noodles according to the package instructions until they are al dente. Drain and set aside.

  2. Cook Chicken:In a large skillet or wok, heat some vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Add the thinly sliced chicken and cook until it's no longer pink. Remove the chicken from the pan and set it aside.

  3. Saute Aromatics: In the same pan, add a bit more oil if needed. Saut√© minced garlic and ginger for about 30 seconds until fragrant.

  4. Add Vegetables: Add the chopped vegetables to the pan and stir-fry until they begin to soften.

  5. Prepare Broth or Sauce: Depending on your preference, you can either add chicken broth to the pan for a soupy version or create a sauce using soy sauce and a small amount of sesame oil for added flavor.

  6. Combine Ingredients:Return the cooked chicken to the pan and mix it with the vegetables and sauce or broth.

  7. Scramble Eggs: Push the ingredients to one side of the pan and add a bit of oil to the empty space. Crack the eggs into the pan and scramble them until they are fully cooked.

  8. Add Noodles: Add the cooked egg noodles to the pan and toss everything together until the noodles are coated with the sauce or broth and the chicken and vegetables are evenly distributed.

  9. Season: Season the chicken noodles with salt and pepper to taste. Adjust the soy sauce or sesame oil if needed to suit your flavor preferences.

  10. Serve: Transfer the chicken noodles to serving bowls, garnish with sliced green onions if desired, and enjoy your comforting and flavorful meal!


Chicken 65

Chicken 65 is a popular and spicy South Indian fried chicken dish known for its intense flavors and crispiness. Here's a brief description of the recipe:

List Of Ingredients:

  • Boneless chicken pieces, typically thigh or breast

  • Yogurt

  • Ginger-garlic paste

  • Turmeric powder

  • Red chili powder

  • Garam masala

  • Curry leaves

  • Cornstarch or rice flour

  • Lemon juice

  • Salt to taste

  • Oil for frying

  • Red or green chilies (optional, for extra heat)

Instructions For Preparation

  1. Marinate Chicken: Start by marinating the chicken pieces in a mixture of yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric powder, and salt. Add a squeeze of lemon juice for some tanginess. Allow the chicken to marinate for at least 30 minutes, or you can refrigerate it for a few hours to enhance the flavors.

  2. Coat with Cornstarch: In a separate bowl, combine cornstarch or rice flour with a bit of red chili powder and salt. Coat the marinated chicken pieces evenly with this mixture. This will give the chicken its signature crispy texture.

  3. Fry Chicken: Heat oil in a deep pan or skillet over medium-high heat. Once the oil is hot, carefully add the coated chicken pieces, a few at a time, ensuring they don't overcrowd the pan. Fry until they turn golden brown and crispy. Remove them with a slotted spoon and drain excess oil on paper towels.

  4. Temper with Curry Leaves: In the same pan, you can use a bit of the remaining oil to temper with curry leaves and slit red or green chilies for added flavor and aroma. Be cautious, as this can be quite spicy.

  5. Combine: Return the fried chicken pieces to the pan and toss them in the tempering mixture. This helps the chicken absorb more flavor.

  6. Serve:Transfer the Chicken 65 to a serving plate and garnish with additional curry leaves. It's typically served hot and can be a delicious appetizer or side dish.
